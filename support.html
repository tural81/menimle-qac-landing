Support

We read every message. Most replies go out within 24 hours.

Need help with something specific? The fastest path is the in-app form: open the App, tap Profile → Report a problem, pick A bug, A person, An idea, or Something else, and tell us what happened. The report reaches our admin panel directly.

You can also reach us by email: tural.yusifov@avtostop.tv.

Frequently asked

The app keeps logging me out — what do I do?

Make sure you’re on the latest version (iOS Settings → General → Software Update, then App Store → Run with me → Update). We shipped a fix in early May 2026 that ends the parallel-refresh race that was kicking some Apple-Sign-In users back to the welcome screen.

Heart rate shows "—" while I'm running.

iPhone alone can't read live HR. Wear an Apple Watch with Workout active, or grant Apple Health permission so we can read passive samples. After granting, restart the run.

How do I delete my account?

Profile → Privacy & Data → Delete account. The action removes your runs, profile, photos, and chat history within 30 days. There is no undo.

Calories on the home screen don't match WHOOP.

We pull today's cycle.kilojoule from WHOOP, which is the same number WHOOP shows. If they differ, tap the tile to open the Stats tab — that pulls the latest sample directly.

I can't tap a buddy.

Make sure both of you have completed onboarding (city + a name set). Tapping is enabled the moment that's true.

Still stuck?

Email tural.yusifov@avtostop.tv with the iOS version and app build number (Profile → bottom of the screen). We'll get back to you within 24 hours, weekdays.
