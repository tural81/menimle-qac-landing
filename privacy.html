1. Information we collect

Account & profile

  • Email address (from email signup, Google, or Apple Sign-In).
  • First and last name.
  • Date of birth (used only to compute and display your age — the date itself is never shown publicly).
  • Gender (optional).
  • Home city (entered manually or detected via reverse-geocoding your GPS once at sign-up).
  • Profile photo and bio you upload yourself.
  • Running preferences: pace, environments (street, park, gym, etc.), goals, listening preferences.

Run & activity data

  • GPS coordinates of the route during an outdoor run.
  • Distance, duration, average pace, and step count.
  • Heart rate, active calories, and step samples read from Apple Health (only if you grant permission).
  • Recovery, strain, and sleep summaries from WHOOP (only if you connect your WHOOP account).

Location for buddy matching

  • While the App is in the foreground we may send your current latitude/longitude every five minutes so we can show "Buddies near you" sorted by distance. We never collect background location for this purpose.

Communications

  • Direct messages exchanged with other connected users.
  • Reports you submit about another user.
  • Bug reports / feedback you submit through the “Report a problem” form, including the OS and app version.

Identifiers

  • An internal numeric user ID we assign on signup.
  • An Expo push notification token, used only to deliver run / event / message notifications.

2. How we use your information

  • To create and authenticate your account.
  • To match you with running partners by city, pace, goals, and (if available) physical proximity.
  • To track and save your runs, calories, heart rate, and streaks.
  • To suggest a daily workout based on your WHOOP recovery or Apple Health sleep data.
  • To deliver notifications about messages, run invites, and events you opted into.
  • To respond to your support / feedback submissions.
  • To detect abuse, spam, or fraud and to enforce our Terms.

3. Sharing and disclosure

We do not sell your personal data and we do not share it with advertising networks or data brokers. Limited disclosure happens only as follows:

  • Other users: Your name, profile photo, city, age, pace, goals, and (after a connection is accepted) chat messages are visible to runners you match or connect with.
  • Service providers: Our hosting provider (Railway) stores the database; Apple delivers push notifications. They process data on our behalf under contract.
  • Legal: If required by law, court order, or to protect rights, property, or safety.

4. Health and Location data

Health metrics (heart rate, active calories, steps, sleep, workouts) are read from Apple HealthKit and/or WHOOP only after you grant permission. They stay on your account; they are never sold and are never used for advertising.

GPS during a run is captured to build your route map and to compute distance / pace; while you are not running, only a low-frequency foreground ping is used to power the "Buddies near you" feature. You can revoke location and Health permissions at any time in iOS Settings, and we will silently fall back to manual options.

5. Data retention & deletion

We keep your data for as long as your account exists. You can:

  • Export your data from inside the App (Profile → Privacy & Data).
  • Delete your account and all linked data from the same screen, or by emailing us. This action is permanent.

6. Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights under GDPR (EU/UK), CCPA (California), or similar laws to access, correct, port, or delete your personal data, and to object to or restrict certain processing. To exercise these rights, use the in-app controls or email us at the address below — we will respond within 30 days.

7. Security

All traffic between the App and our servers is encrypted with TLS 1.2+. Passwords are hashed with bcrypt; we never see them in plaintext. Refresh tokens rotate on every use and are bound to the device. No system is perfectly secure, but we work hard to protect your information.

8. Children

The App is not directed to children under 10. Our onboarding refuses sign-ups for anyone under that age. If you believe a child has provided us personal data, contact us and we will delete it.

9. Changes to this policy

If we make material changes to this Privacy Policy we will update the “Last updated” date above and, where appropriate, notify you in the App or by email before the changes take effect.
